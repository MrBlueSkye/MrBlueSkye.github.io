A Collection of my Study Notes

Welcome, friends! My name is Skylar, and I am a Network Engineer and a tech enthusiast. In my journey for knowledge I have studied a whole range of technologies and filled notebook after notebook with...well...notes. But they're good notes! Great notes! Notes so great I just couldn't bear to keep them to myself! So here you go, friends, my notes are yours! I have created this space not only for you, but primarily for me. For now we venture onwards, post haste...

Networking Technologies

Computer Networking is the process of transferring information from one Network Device to another. Using uniform communication protocols that all devices can interpret, humanity has created the Internet, a Global Platorm of shared information and knowledge and undoubtedly one of the largest technological acheivements of our era. Here we will delve into these communication protocols and make sense of how these technologies operate.

Linux and Server Administration

Networking Devices together means little without the neccessary Hardware and Software at each endpoint to communicate from and to. The Linux Kernel began as an open-source community-driven project to build an operating system flexible enough to work with any computing hardware, and to say it acheived this would be an understatement. Different flavors of Linux will be used on almost any device nowadays, from Internet Web Servers and ultra-powerful supercomputers, to smaller computers such as the Smartphone in your pocket, or the TV in your living room. Here I will dip a toe into the expansive world of Linux.

Blockchain and Cryptocurrencies

Perhaps the most revolutionary technological paradigm shift in recent history, the concept of decentralized, consensus-based Blockchains will begin to be applied to almost every aspect of modern society, in an internet of Value and Truth. Here we will cover Blockchain, Bitcoin, Consensus-based algorithms and how this technolocgy will change our digital world.
